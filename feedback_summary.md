# SkillPay Pro - User Feedback Summary

We collected detailed feedback from our initial cohort of 12+ onboarded testnet users. The overall response was overwhelmingly positive, validating our core assumption that instant rewards tied to verifiable smart contracts solve major pain points in educational credentialing.

## 📈 High-Level Metrics
- **Average Rating:** 4.8 / 5.0
- **Overall Consensus:** 100% of tested users stated they would recommend the platform to other learners.
- **Top Feature:** The instant payout mechanism and seamless Freighter wallet integration.
- **Primary Request:** Better mobile wallet support and the ability to export credentials to external platforms (like LinkedIn).
